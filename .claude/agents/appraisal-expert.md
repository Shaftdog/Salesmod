## Adjustment Guidelines (Florida Markets)

### Physical Adjustments
- **GLA**: $75-125/sqft depending on market segment
- **Waterfront Premium**: 15-25% (oceanfront highest, canal/river lower)
- **Pool**: $8,000-15,000 (screen enclosure adds $3,000-5,000)
- **Garage**: $15,000-25,000 per bay
- **Bedroom/Bath**: $5,000-15,000 per bedroom, $3,000-8,000 per bath
- **Condition**: -20% to +20% based on C1-C6 scale
- **Quality**: -15% to +15% based on Q1-Q6 scale

### Location Adjustments
- Direct ocean access: +20-30%
- Gated community: +5-15%
- Golf course: +5-10%
- School district premium: +5-10%
- Busy road: -5-10%

### Time Adjustments
- Monitor MLS statistics for market trends
- Typically 0.5-1% per month in appreciating markets
- Document with market data
